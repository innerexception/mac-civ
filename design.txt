mac-civ: the interesting decisions of building a civ without the boring bits

resources: 
	military, sociology, economics, culture
alignment: 
	freedom/authoritarian, communal/individualist

events:
	you might meet another civ on your planet or otherwise
	moral technological choices
	become a vassal of a powerful but benevoltent empire
	be lucky and blessed with great natural resources

interface:
	text blurb with 3 or more choices, each listing a +/- to a category

will you be prepared for the reckoning?

choices become available if you have the required resource level and/or alignment
choices can add/subtract from your resource levels, and also your alignment
